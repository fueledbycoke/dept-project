ELDEN RING DLC

While news regarding Elden Ring's upcoming DLC, Shadow of the Erdtree, has been nonexistent past the initial reveal teaser, there are still a few big events left in 2023 where the DLC could make its first large-scale showing. First announced in February 2023, Shadow of the Erdtree was revealed with nothing more than a mysterious teaser image that gave relatively few hints towards what the DLC might be about. However, no new information about the DLC has been revealed since, and developer FromSoftware has yet to show any real trailers. With Shadow of the Erdtree in development, Elden Ring follows in the footsteps of many of FromSoftware's past games, such as Dark Souls and Bloodborne, many of which have received significant DLC expansions that build on the mysteries and lore of their respective games - and in most cases, are considered among the best of the developer's creations. The quality of FromSoftware's past work, as well as the extreme success of Elden Ring itself, mean that Shadow of the Erdtree has very high standards to live up to upon its eventual release. But while this likely means a long development time, mirroring that of base Elden Ring, there are still a few opportunities for the DLC to show itself off in 2023.
